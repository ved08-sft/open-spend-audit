# Unit Economics & Financial Modeling — SpendOptima

If Credex deploys SpendOptima tomorrow, the financial viability of this B2B lead-generation asset is modeled as follows.

---

## 1. Converted Lead Value to Credex (Customer Lifetime Value - LTV)
Credex sells discounted enterprise AI infrastructure credits (Cursor, Claude, OpenAI). We monetize by taking a **15% spread** on the volume of credits transacted.

### Converted Lead Profile (Seed Startup):
- **Average Monthly AI Spend**: $2,500/month (Seats + raw API tokens).
- **Average Savings secured via Credex credits**: 30% discount.
- **Startup Monthly Credex Credit Volume**: $1,750/month (Startup pays Credex, Credex provisions the credits).
- **Credex Monthly Gross Revenue (15% spread)**: $1,750 * 15% = $262.50/month.
- **Average Customer Retention (Startups stay for at least 18 months before graduation)**: 18 months.
- **Gross Profit Margin on transactions**: 100% (zero inventory cost; software facilitation).
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: $262.50/mo * 18 months = **$4,725**.

---

## 3. Funnel Conversion Rates & Profitability Modeling
We model our lead-generation funnel to identify the baseline conversion thresholds required to secure profitability. 

### Target Funnel Ratios:
1. **Audit Completed**: 1,000 visitors (Cost: Blended hosting and serverless API fees).
2. **Lead Captured (Email submission to unlock AI CFO summary)**: 500 leads (50% conversion rate).
3. **High-Savings Cases Detected (Savings > $500/mo)**: 100 audits (20% of leads).
4. **Credex consultation Booked**: 20 founders (20% of high-savings cases).
5. **Credit Purchase / Client Converted**: 5 customers (25% conversion of booked calls).

### Profitability Math:
- **Total cost to run 1,000 audits**:
  - Netlify / Vercel Serverless Hosting: $20.00
  - Gemini 2.5 API token fees (500 summaries * $0.002): $1.00
  - Resend transactional email fees (500 sends): $0.00 (Free Tier)
  - **Total Variable Operating Cost**: **$21.00**
- **Revenue generated from 1,000 audits**:
  - 5 converted customers * $4,725 LTV = **$23,625**.
- **Runway ROI**: $23,625 / $21.00 = **1,125x return on variable cost**.
- **Minimum Converted Rate for Profitability**: Even if our conversion from *Audit Completed* -> *Converted Client* drops to **0.01%** (1 in 10,000), the high value of a single converted startup ($4,725 LTV) completely covers the serverless variable hosting overhead, making SpendOptima a highly high-margin asset.

## 4. The Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To drive **$1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** in 18 months, let's calculate what must be true.

### The Math:
- **$1M ARR** = $83,333 gross monthly revenue for Credex.
- With an average gross commission of **$262.50/month per startup**, Credex needs **318 active, transacting startups** on its platform.
- Over an **18-month window**, Credex must acquire an average of **18 new startups per month** (318 / 18).

### The Funnel Scaling Blueprint (To acquire 18 startups/mo):
- **Audits required per month**:
  - With a 1% conversion from *Audit Completed* -> *Converted Client*, we need **1,800 completed audits per month** (60 audits per day).
- **Leads captured per month**:
  - With a 50% capture rate, this yields **900 business email leads per month**.
- **GTM Scaling Actions**:
  1. Standardize partnerships with **50 active Fractional CFOs** who run SpendOptima as a default onboarding tool for every new seed client.
  2. Embed the free SpendOptima widget into **10 VC portfolio portals**, automating audits for all newly funded startups.
  3. Continuous viral social outreach on X/LinkedIn by sharing anonymized reports showing how specific teams saved $800+/mo.
