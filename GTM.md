# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM) — SpendOptima

## 1. The Target User Persona
Our exact target user is the **VP of Engineering** or **Head of Operations / Chief of Staff** at seed-stage to Series A technology startups (15 to 75 employees). 

### Why this specific persona?
- **VP of Engineering / CTO**: They are responsible for setting up developer environments and tooling, but as the engineering team scales, they suddenly realize they are paying retail for multiple overlapping editors (e.g., Cursor, Windsurf, Copilot) and raw APIs. They want to maximize developer velocity but are highly sensitive to unnecessary capital leakage.
- **Chief of Staff / Head of Ops**: They do not write code, but they manage the monthly credit card invoices. They lack the engineering context to judge if "Opus API" is necessary or if "o1 API" is over-provisioned, but they desperately want a clear checklist to challenge engineering spend.

## 4. The 30-Day $0 Launch Playbook (First 100 Users)
We will execute an organic, high-value content engine to secure our first 100 audited users in 30 days:
- **Days 1–7 (The "Brutal Audit" Content Campaign)**:
  We will publish a series of short, highly analytical breakdowns on X/LinkedIn dissecting the pricing traps of major AI vendors (e.g., *"How a single developer on Claude Teams pays $150/mo instead of $20/mo"*). We will back these claims with precise math, cite `PRICING_DATA.md`, and drop SpendOptima as a free tool to calculate their specific leakage.
- **Days 8–15 (Direct Fractional CFO DM Outreach)**:
  Fractional CFOs manage finances for 5 to 15 startups concurrently. We will cold-reach out to 30 Fractional CFOs on LinkedIn offering SpendOptima as a white-labeled or free assessment tool to run for their portfolio companies. Auditing one CFO's client base instantly nets us 10+ startups.
- **Days 16–25 (The YC/Incubator Pitch)**:
  Post a high-value thread on YC Bookface and founder Slacks outlining the *"Ecosystem Redundancy Rating"*. We will offer to run instant audits for founders, highlighting that seed startups are leaking an average of $350/mo on AI seats alone.
- **Days 26–30 (Launch on Product Hunt & Kernal)**:
  Launch the tool under a catchy name like "CFO-AI-Audit" on Product Hunt, targeting the "SaaS Management" category.
