## 4. Executive B2B FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

### Q1: Why is SpendOptima free?
**A:** SpendOptima is a free tool powered by **Credex**. Our goal is to genuinely help startup founders and engineering teams identify software waste. For teams that have significant savings opportunities, we surface Credex as a partner to help them secure discounted bulk AI credits (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise). It’s a pure win-win: you locate the leak for free, and we help you plug it at a discount.

### Q2: What is a "Ghost Seat" trap?
**A:** Many AI SaaS vendors enforce minimum seat tiers. For instance, Claude's Team plan mandates a minimum of 5 seats ($150/mo). If you only have 2 active team members on the plan, you are paying $90/mo for 3 completely empty "ghost seats." SpendOptima auto-detects these seat floors and provides step-by-step downgrade guides to individual Pro accounts.

### Q3: What is "Model Arbitrage" and how does it save me money?
**A:** Startups frequently use expensive reasoning models (like OpenAI's o1 or Anthropic's Opus) for automated background tasks like JSON database sorting or simple text parsing. These queries do not require deep reasoning. By shifting those simple pipelines to low-cost metered endpoints like Google Gemini 2.5 Flash, you maintain identical output quality at a **95% to 96% reduction in structural API cost**.

### Q4: How is my audit data handled? Is it public?
**A:** Your calculations are stored privately. When you create a shareable report URL, **all identifying details (email, company name, role) are completely stripped from the database payload**. Only anonymized tool inputs and savings numbers are visible on the public report page. We enforce strict data privacy standards.

### Q5: What is the "Realism Coefficient" used in the calculations?
**A:** In real companies, migrating engineers away from their preferred editor (e.g. from Windsurf to Cursor) introduces friction and loss of productivity. SpendOptima does not claim 100% savings for tool overlaps. We apply a **70% realism coefficient** to our redundancy math, retaining 30% as a migration friction buffer. This makes our audit reports highly defensible, realistic, and CFO-approved.
